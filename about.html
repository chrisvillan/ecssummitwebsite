Our History

The first Leadership & Diversity Summit was hosted by ACM & ACM-W last year on April 29th, 2017.

Alvaro Riveraz, the former president of Association for Computing Machinery, and Sara Lipowsky the former president of Association for Computing Machinery - Women led a team to design and host this opportunity for students. The purpose was to promote diversity and prepare our students with necessary leadership skills to succeed in the workforce.

Our team is devoted to make your experience as beneficial as it can be.
